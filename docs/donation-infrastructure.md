# dot-self — Donation & Sustainability

**Sub-Spec C**
**Part of dot-self / AIS**

---

## Principles

- **0% to operator.** All donations go to infrastructure — domain registration, hosting for landing page, future framework ports, HF Space inference costs.
- **Privacy-respecting.** ProtonMail for contact. ETH for direct support. BuyMeACoffee for low-friction contributions.
- **Transparent.** No VC dependency. No lock-in. No business model — the protocol is MIT.

## Mechanisms

### 1. ETH Wallet Address
Direct blockchain transfer. No platform fees. Global.

### 2. BuyMeACoffee
Familiar UX. Micro-donations. Supports recurring contributions if available.

### 3. ProtonMail Contact
Privacy-respecting communication. ProtonMail link in README for sponsorship inquiries, partnership discussions, or questions.

## What Donations Fund

| Item | Est. Cost | Priority |
|------|-----------|----------|
| `aetheris.dev` or `dot-self.org` domain | ~$15/yr | High |
| HF Space inference for demo | ~$0-9/mo (PRO tier) | Medium |
| Landing page hosting (GitHub Pages / Vercel) | Free | High |
| Future framework ports (CrewAI, LangGraph, etc.) | Time | Medium |

## Operator Commitment

Operator manages the wallet, ProtonMail, and BuyMeACoffee account. All proceeds transparently support infrastructure. Operator covers their own time and costs.
