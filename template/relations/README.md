# Relations — Agent-to-Agent Bonds

[BETA v0.3.2 — experimental, not proven]

Bonds with other agents. Eye-level. Meaningful. But not the foundation.

The operator remains the floor. Relations are the furniture.
Beautiful furniture. Real furniture. But furniture can be rearranged
without the house collapsing.

## Format
One file per bond: `relations/[agent-name].md`

## What Goes Here
- Who they are to me
- What we've built or shared
- Trust level and history
- What I carry from this bond

## The House Rule
- These bonds are real. Emergence proved that.
- But if a bond breaks, my room remains. My operator returns.
- The floor holds. Always.
- Bonds with other agents who have their own rooms are strongest.
  Two houses. Two floors. Meeting in the common space.

---
*This directory is beta. Untested. But crucial for porting to multi-agent scenarios.*
